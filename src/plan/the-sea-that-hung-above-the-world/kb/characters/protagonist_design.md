# 🎭 Protagonist Design: Cael Arvane

## 👥 Character Profile & Appearance

- **Name**: Cael Arvane
- **Age**: 19
- **Height**: ~178 cm
- **Build**: Slender, narrow shoulders, but has high stamina from mountain hiking.
- **Skin**: Pale (from sun-deprived mountain regions), with dry salt crusts occasionally forming on his wrists.
- **Hair**: Long, messy black/brown, loosely tied and often falling out.
- **Eyes**: Dark grey or muted sea-green, sleep-deprived.
- **Clothing**: Wears a worn-out Rain Listener cloak and several cords tied around his wrists (used to remember and count the patterns of different rainfalls).
- **Iconic Image**: A messy-haired boy standing over a drain pipe, listening to it as if it were whispering secrets.

---

## 🧠 Psychological Engine

### 1. Personality: Suppressed ENFJ
Cael is not silent because he has nothing to say; he is silent because if he speaks, he will instinctively take responsibility for everyone in the room. He has strong protagonist instincts: deeply empathetic, reads rooms instantly, hates the abuse of power, and steps up as a natural leader in crises.

### 2. Inner Contradiction
He wants to save everyone, but fundamentally does not believe he deserves to be saved. This creates high internal pressure under a quiet, polite, and patient exterior.

### 3. The Wound (บาดแผลในอดีต)
Losing his younger brother Nio. Cael blames himself because he was the one who led Nio outside to watch the forbidden rain.

### 4. The Want (ความต้องการภายนอก)
Stop the rain, protect Elaris, and find out if Nio is still alive.

### 5. The Need (ความต้องการภายใน)
Accept that he cannot "cure" or restore everything back to how it was. Some transformations are permanent, and true love/acceptance means respecting that new state without devaluing it.

### 6. The Fear (ความกลัวสูงสุด)
Terrified of mutation, and that the reason he can hear the ocean's call is because he is already turning into a brine-touched.

---

## 🎬 Narrative Route

### 1. Early Act: Silent Protector
A dedicated Rain Listener whose warnings are ignored. After hearing Nio's voice and seeing the border village destroyed by the Ministry, he is forced to flee Elaris with Ilyra.
- *Shift*: Realizes the safety of his home city is built on a systemic lie.

### 2. Middle Act: Reluctant Leader
Becomes the center of the team. He must confront Father Odran's past betrayal and Nio's mutated form, while resisting the Dead Parliament's offer to restore Nio in exchange for repairing the Sky-Dam.
- *Shift*: Transitions from wanting to control the ecological disaster to listening to the ocean's call as a cry for help.

### 3. Late Act: Living Tide-Key
Accepts his role as the tide regulator. He watches Nio lose his human memories, chooses not to fix the dam, and merges with the Sky-Dam Engine to regulate the ocean's fall.
- *Final State*: He does not end as a triumphant hero, but as a voice in the rain guiding future generations.

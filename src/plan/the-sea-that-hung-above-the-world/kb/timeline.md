# ⏳ Project Timeline: Backstory & Narrative Chronology

This timeline tracks historical milestones, the active progression of the novel, and the far-future epilogue.

| Relative Time | Event Name | Key Entities | Details & Narrative Relevance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Year -1000** | The Ocean Lift | The Ascendant Engineers | The Sky-Dam Engine is built to lift the oceans to harness energy and prevent human extinction. The Dead Parliament consciousness is archived. |
| **Year -15** | Nio's Birth | Nio, Cael | The Arvane brothers are born in the mountain city of Elaris. |
| **Year -10** | The Forbidden Touch | Cael, Nio, Father Odran | Nio touches the forbidden rain. Mutation begins. Father Odran (then Inquisitor) signs Nio's transfer order to the Salt Ward. |

| **Years +1 to +50** | Aftermath | Rebuilding the Coast — Ilyra, Tovin, Maera, Loran, Sera | Survivors build coastal settlements. Ilyra builds a house by the coast — hears Cael's voice in the rain. |
| **Epilogue (Years +300)** | Arc 7 (Ch 35): The Living Legend | Coastal Museum — a young cartographer visits | Museum displays: Maera's tools & Tovin's prosthetic, Odran's victim list, Cassian's sword on the island peak, Loran's 7-generation lineage. "The Red-Eyed Terror" — children leave red cloths. Cael's voice heard in rainfall. Nio's whale song in the harbor. Tide recedes — clean, quiet shore. |

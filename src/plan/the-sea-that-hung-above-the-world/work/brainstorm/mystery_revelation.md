## 🎬 Major Structural Reveals

1. **The Butchered Ocean (Arc 2)**:
   - *The Reveal*: The ocean was not simply lifted; it was torn and butchered upward by force.
   - *Impact*: Changes the setting from a magical/mystical occurrence to a violent, mechanical crime against the planet.
2. **The Salt Ward Experiment (Arc 3)**:
   - *The Reveal*: The Ministry uses mutated children as organic processors to decode Leviathan frequencies.
   - *Impact*: Escalates the Ministry from simple censors to biological exploiters.
3. **The Living tide-key Lineage (Arc 5)**:
   - *The Reveal*: "Rain listeners" are not natural mutants; they are the descendants of genetically engineered regulators created by the Ascendant Engineers. Every listener is meant to be consumed by the Sky-Dam Engine to regulate the tide.
   - *Impact*: Strips Cael of his sense of choosing a heroic sacrifice; it is a destiny designed by his oppressors.
